Tricopter Redesign and ideas moving on.

This is a continuation of this post a few weeks ago

Since my last post I've redesigned the entire midframe, adding a camera mount and removable battery compartment and making it easier to store electronics inside the frame which is a big plus in rainy weather (which we get a lot of in Normandy, where I am right now)

Rendering of the current assembly.

I'm still using the motor mounts and feet from the thingiverse page files that I used last time, but I hope to replace those soon as they stick out more than I would like, and using piano wire to attach the back motor gimbal seems somewhat redundant when I could be driving it directly
