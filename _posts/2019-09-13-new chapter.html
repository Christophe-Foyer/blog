News about my whereabouts, my summer, my current trajectory.

A lot has changed in the past few months, going from being employed with what was arguably the wrong role, to willfully unemployed with no concrete plans apart from a vague "maybe a masters, probably move to a new country" to now being admitted to a university I thought I had no chance of getting into.

This summer has been very beneficial to me, letting me explore what I'm genuinely interested in, work on developing skills, fix habits and develop new ones. It's been great exploring London, getting used to going to events, which is something I never really had the opportunity to do outside of a university setting due to my previous locations. I've exercised excessively, to the point of worrying about me knees, and for some reason went back for more, trading a few kilometers per day running for Salsa and climbing.

This summer has been exciting, boring, amazing, and disappointing, and while right now it's hard to feel excited about what's coming next, I'm sure I'll enjoy what's to come once I get started.
